Plans to develop an international Dark Sky Reserve in the region are well underway. The opportunities are potential for growth and investment created by the reserve are significant. Meet Becky from Under The Stars to find out more about astronomy in the Wairarapa and get involved in creation of the Dark Sky Economic Plan at this community meeting.
