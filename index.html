Star Gazing / Astronomy

Under The Stars

Under The Stars: Wairarapa Astronomy is an astronomy tour business providing stargazing experiences, consultations and education to Wairarapa venues, special events and festivals. Under The Stars aims to educate schools, heritage institutions and visitors about astronomy and the value of the night sky.
