An award winning business needs brilliant staff. That is exactly what we have!
Meet them here, or say hi when you are next at an event.

Becky Bateman

Becky started Under The Stars in 2018 when she moved her family over to the Wairarapa to be under the Wairarapa's Dark Skies. She has a background in teaching, space outreach and loves science. She loves being out and about, creating new experiences and including the community in astronomy. She is a dedicated committee member of the Wairarapa Dark Sky Society, aiming to certify the area as a Dark Sky Reserve.

Mike

Mike watched the Moon landing when he was young and it was a life changing event.

Brian Cox's series 'Star Gazing' really got him fired up to learn more about our solar system and the universe beyond, so our own galaxy the Milky Way was a great place to start.

Mike’s background is in leadership and organisational development. He has a Masters Degree in Business Coaching, as well as Diplomas in Training & Assessment and Multi-Media Design.

Rachel

Rachel made the move to the Wairarapa with her family in January 2017. She and her husband and two small children live on a small farm near Martinborough running a few sheep and some rogue chooks.

Rachel is a self-confessed nature nerd and has worked for many years with community and school groups delivering environmental education and learning outside the classroom programmes.

She’s always on the lookout for the ‘teachable moment’ (much to her daughter’s vexation at times) is thrilled to be part of the Under The Stars team guiding you through the magical and magnificent Wairarapa night sky.

Juanita

Juanita has been fascinated by the night sky since staring up at Halley’s comet as a child. This led to a passion for the stories hidden behind the constellations, and a love of the way the stars have always guided us home.

Charlotte

Gazing in wonder at the stars above is a regular part of life since moving to the Wairarapa five years ago.

The dark sky project is something I am extremely passionate about and it is so exciting to be involved and be part of the Under the Stars team.

She is passionate not only about learning more about the wonder of the sky above but also in protecting, educating, and inspiring people to realise this is so incredibly special and not something to be taken for granted.

If you have a starry project in mind, contact Charlotte on charlotte@underthestars.co.nz.

Scott

Scott's worked in schools, science centres, museums and now libraries and love to talk about science.

When he's not at a library or pointing people to the stars he loves to play hockey, walk, run, bike and generally just being outdoors exploring our region.

"My earliest astronomy memory was waiting up late to see Halley's Comet as a 4 year old, only to be denied by clouds! However, from then on I have always been looking at the stars, wondering what else I may one day spot!”
