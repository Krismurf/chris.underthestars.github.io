Martinborough’s Dark Sky Society

Under The Stars is proud to be involved with Martinborough’s Dark Sky Society and has been an active member since early 2018.

The society’s aims are to preserve and protect the night sky as well as informing the community about how to engage and educate visitors about the Dark Sky Reserve in Martinborough.

Find out more on their website
