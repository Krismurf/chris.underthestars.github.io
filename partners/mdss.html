Wairarapa Dark Sky Society
(Previously Martinborough’s Dark Sky Society)

Under The Stars is proud to be involved with Wairarapa Dark Sky Society and has been an active member since early 2018.

The society’s aims are to preserve and protect the night sky as well as informing the community about how to engage and educate visitors about the Dark Sky Reserve in the Wairarapa.
