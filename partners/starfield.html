Under The Stars at Starfield Martinborough

John and the Team are putting the final touches to StarField, a dedicated space for Astronomy in Martinborough. Hidden deep in the proposed Wairarapa Dark Sky reserve the skies are simply stunning. Under the Stars has partnered up with StarField to offer regular astronomy sessions on a well equipped purpose built site for observing celestial objects.
