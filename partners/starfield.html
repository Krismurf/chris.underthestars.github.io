Under The Stars at Star Field Martinborough

John and the Team are putting the final touches to Star Field, a dedicated space for Astronomy in Martinborough. Hidden deep in the proposed Wairarapa Dark Sky reserve the skies are simply stunning.

Under the Stars has partnered up with Star Field to offer regular astronomy sessions on a well equipped purpose built site for observing celestial objects.
